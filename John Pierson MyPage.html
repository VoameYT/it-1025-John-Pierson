Cavs 2016 3-1 Comeback, Cavaliers end Cleveland's long championship drought.

LeBron James makes good on his promise as the Cavaliers win the 2016 NBA Finals in epic fashion. The Cavs top the Warriors in a Game 7 thriller, becoming the first NBA team to erase a 3-1 Finals deficit. Many people say this was the best finals ever and one of the best single player playoff domination by Lebron James.
